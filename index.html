Privacy Policy

Effective date: 01 May 2025

App name: Orama Tarot

Developer: Vitaleo Naturals SL

Orama Tarot ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Orama Tarot (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Data: Name and email address.
  • Non-Personal Data: App usage, device model, OS, language, and country.

We do not collect sensitive personal or financial information directly. All purchases are handled securely via Google Play and RevenueCat.

2. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Consent: For sending push notifications, showing personalized ads, and analytics tracking.
  • Performance of a Contract: To provide you with tarot readings and app features you request.
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve app performance and understand user behavior, while respecting your rights.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide personalized tarot readings
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To analyze usage via analytics tools like PostHog
  • To send optional push notifications
  • To display ads and measure ad performance using AdMob

4. Third-Party Services

We may share information with the following third-party services:

  • Supabase – for secure user authentication and storage
  • RevenueCat – for managing subscriptions and purchases
  • PostHog – for anonymized usage analytics
  • Expo Notifications – for sending scheduled messages
  • AdMob – for displaying advertisements and collecting data related to ad performance

5. Advertising and Advertising ID

We use Google AdMob to show ads in our app. AdMob may use your device’s Advertising ID to provide personalized ads and to track ad performance. This identifier is managed by Google Play Services and can be reset or disabled in your device settings.

6. Consent for EU Users (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we use a Consent Management Platform (CMP) to collect and manage your consent preferences before serving personalized ads. You can update your consent choices at any time from within the app settings or when prompted on app launch.

7. Opt-Out of Personalized Advertising

If you prefer not to receive personalized ads, you can opt out by changing your device settings:

  • Android: Go to Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization.
  • iOS: Go to Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising > Limit Ad Tracking.

8. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are located in the EEA, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • The right to access the personal data we hold about you
  • The right to request correction or deletion of your data
  • The right to object to or restrict our processing of your data
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to withdraw your consent at any time
  • The right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@oramatarot.com

9. Data Retention

We keep data only as long as necessary to provide the service or comply with legal requirements.

10. Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
